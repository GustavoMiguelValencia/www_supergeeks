Project Description

GeekSurvey will bridge the gap between researchers and participants. It will be a simple web application with accounts for two types of users: Researchers and Participants. Researchers will be able to create studies that the Participants can participate in if their profile is within the researchers desired demographic. This will provide opportunity for participants and solve the problem that researchers often have with obtaining participants.
